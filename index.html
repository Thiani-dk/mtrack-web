01.

Fort Knox Privacy (100% Offline)

Your Data Stays Yours

M-Track does not have internet access. It is physically impossible for the app to send your financial data to the cloud, to us, or to anyone else. Everything is processed and stored securely right here on your device.

02.

The Scanning Engine

Laser-Focused on Mobile Money

We ignore regular texts and spam. The engine is trained exclusively to read M-PESA and Airtel Money receipts. When you first install the app, it looks back through the last 6 months of your inbox to build your financial history instantly, tracking spending in neat 30-day cycles.

03.

Smart Learning

An App That Learns From You

Sometimes the app might not know what a transaction was for. When you manually assign a category, M-Track will ask if you want to create a 'Smart Rule'. Say yes, and it automatically remembers and categorizes all future payments to that specific person or business.

04.

Built-In Calculators

Salary & VAT Math Made Easy

Salary Calculator: Wondering what your actual take-home pay is? This tool factors in all current Kenyan tax laws—including PAYE, SHIF, Housing Levy, and NSSF.

VAT Calculator: Easily add 16% tax to an amount, or reverse-engineer a receipt to find the base price.

05.

Pro-Level Exporting

Tidy CSV Reports

Whether you need to submit expenses to your boss, or you're a data nerd who wants to plug your spending into Excel or Python, our Export feature handles it. Select specific transactions and generate a perfectly clean, machine-readable CSV file straight to your Downloads folder.
